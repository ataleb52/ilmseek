Ilm Seek gives young Muslims the opportunity to travel and visit other communities, get exposed to new ideas, and gain knowledge in areas of Islamic Studies, or any other subject that might benefit their community. The program gives them to learn Islamic Studies from renowned scholars and open their minds to new ideas, all while giving them the opportunity to put there new skills and knowledge to work benefiting their community.

Ready to seek Ilm?

The Philosophy

Knowledge

The Ilm Seek Project is a means of exposing young Muslims to communities outside of their hometowns, allowing them to learn Islamic Studies from renowned scholars, and open their minds to new ideas, all while giving their community the opportunity to benefit from their newfound knowledge.

How?

Travel

Ilm Seek is open to all young Muslims in the Tucson community between the ages of 16-30. However those selected for scholarships will show a motivation to learn and make a change in their community. These Ilm Seekers are willing to put their faith in Allah, roll up their sleeves, and get to work on improving not only the Muslim community, but the surrounding ones as well.
